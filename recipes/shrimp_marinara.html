Shrimp Marinara


Go back

Description

"This is great for a quick weeknight meal, but I could also see it as a candlelight dinner for two," recipe developer Juliana Hale says of this shrimp marinara recipe.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces dry spaghetti
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1 (15-ounce) diced fire-roasted tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil, plus more for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

Steps

  1. Prepare spaghetti according to package directions; drain and keep warm.
  2. Meanwhile, heat oil over medium heat in a large skillet. Add shrimp and cook 2 to 3 minutes or until nearly opaque, turning once.
  3. Transfer shrimp to a plate. Add garlic and crushed red pepper to the skillet. Cook and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Stir in undrained tomatoes, tomato sauce, basil, balsamic vinegar, oregano, and salt. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, until thickened, 10 minutes.
  5. Return shrimp to skillet and simmer to heat through, about 1 minute. Serve over spaghetti, topped with additional fresh basil.
